Lebron James

James will be rested for Wednesday's game against the Pacers, Dave McMenamin of ESPN reports.
Spin: On the second night of a back-to-back set, James will rest as the Cavaliers work to manage his minutes with the regular season nearing its end. Cleveland has not clinched the top seed in the East, but it holds a 3.5-game lead over Toronto entering Wednesday night. Last time James rested, eight days ago versus Houston, Iman Shumpert entered the starting lineup at small forward, so that's likely the direction in which coach Tyronn Lue will again lean.
